About this car

This 2008 SLK350 is a blacked-out hardtop convertible — black paint over a black interior, riding on aftermarket black wheels. The 3.5-liter V6 sends power through a 7-speed automatic, and the folding hardtop tucks itself completely out of sight into the trunk, which remains usable. It runs, drives, and stops, and it turns heads everywhere it goes.

The previous chapter of this car involved real money and care: it was purchased for around $13,000, with roughly another $7,000 put into dialing it in. The sound system alone — the professionally installed JVC unit with CarPlay, Bluetooth, and backup camera — represents a meaningful chunk of that investment. Outside of the stereo and wheels, the car is stock.

Comparable SLK350s in similar condition and mileage have averaged roughly $12,000–$13,000 over the past 24 months according to cars.com. This one is priced to move and the seller is open to serious offers. Located in Eugene, Oregon. No trades.
